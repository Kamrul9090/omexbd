WELCOME TO OMEX EXPRESS.

International Express, Freight & Logistics Services

“OMEX” is a brand name of Overseas Mail Express. It was founded in the year of 2011 and registered under the Trade Mark authority. The inception of OMEX evolves from the importance of transportation of documents and parcels. Despite the advancement of communication technology, the importance of courier service is still enormous. Understanding this importance we have developed our delivery network ourselves and chose the world-leading courier service providers like DHL, UPS, FedEx, SF, BLUEDART, ARAMEX, and many operators as our delivery agents depending on their delivery strength in the destination.

We have developed associated offices in Dubai, Singapore, China, and India as an interrogator. Having the long experience and capability to use the service of these major couriers, we have gathered a vast experience about the strength and weakness on service and price factor of outbound shipment for both documents and parcels to all over the world.

Over the period, we have set up our own channel infrastructure and developed a dedicated service channel that is well capable to meet the customers demand fully. We have well-trained group of staffs, experienced in their respective fields.
